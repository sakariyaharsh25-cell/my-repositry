Our Story

TorridWave was founded with a vision to help businesses leverage the full potential of Microsoft SharePoint and Power Platform. We specialize in developing custom solutions using Power Apps, Power BI, SPFx, Power Automate, Microsoft Forms, and Workflow technologies.

Our team of Microsoft Experts Professionals has been delivering innovative SharePoint products and services for over a decade. We've helped hundreds of organizations transform their business processes, enhance collaboration, and drive digital transformation through Microsoft 365 solutions.

We believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust, technical excellence, and a deep understanding of Microsoft SharePoint ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with Microsoft SharePoint and Power Platform solutions that drive growth, enhance productivity, and create competitive advantages. We help organizations unlock the full potential of Microsoft 365 through custom Power Apps, Power BI dashboards, SPFx solutions, and automated workflows.

We are committed to understanding our clients' unique business challenges and delivering tailored SharePoint solutions that exceed expectations. Our mission is to be your trusted Microsoft SharePoint partner, helping you transform your business processes and achieve digital excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading Microsoft SharePoint and Power Platform consulting partner that businesses trust for innovation, reliability, and transformative solutions. We envision a future where Microsoft 365 seamlessly integrates with business goals to create unprecedented value and drive digital transformation.

We strive to set new standards in SharePoint development through continuous innovation, exceptional service delivery, and a deep commitment to helping our clients succeed with Microsoft technologies. Our vision is to make SharePoint and Power Platform accessible and powerful for businesses of all sizes.
