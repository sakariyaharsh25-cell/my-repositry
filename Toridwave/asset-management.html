Asset Management

Automate, track, and optimize your assets with our comprehensive SharePoint-based solution. Streamline assignments, maintenance, and analytics with fully integrated automated workflows designed for the modern enterprise.

Download Document

Deployment Workflow

Deploy our asset management web part effortlessly within your existing SharePoint environment.

Automated Structure Creation

  • Step 1: Deploy the .sppkg file to the App Catalog seamlessly.
  • Step 2: Add the "Asset Management System" via Site Contents.
  • Step 3: Create a page, insert the Web Part, and hit publish.
  • Step 4: On first load, the system intelligently creates all background lists like Asset Types, Products, Assignments, and History.

Key Features

Robust architecture enforcing deep validation mechanisms.

Asset Type Management

Control categories like Laptops, Monitors, and Mobile Devices. You can easily manage categories and assign them to individual assets. Assets carry smart statuses: Available, In Use, In Maintenance, Retired, Disposed.

Assignment Logistics

Securely allocate inventory. Rigid validations ensure an asset is Available before assignment. Past assignment dates are blocked, and automatic status toggles shift the asset back correctly.

Maintenance Workflow

Track maintenance with an enforced state flow: Planned → In Progress → Completed / Cancelled. Logical cost tracking is featured, proactively denying incorrect resource allocations.

Validations Tracking

Maintains absolute transparency. All interactions—from creation and assignments to final disposal—are actively recorded. Asset deletions are strictly prohibited if possessing active bindings.

Analytics & Reporting Dashboard

Visualize system health, total portfolio value, and utilization securely in one place.

Donut Metrics

Distributes the volume of Available vs In Use assets beautifully. Hover over segments for exact analytical counts.

Bar Comparisons

Compares complex asset statistics or categorical groupings. Very useful to observe category metrics rapidly at scale.

Activity Timelines

Detailed maps trending over flexible timeframes (Week/Month/Year) indicating hardware assignment vs deep maintenance flux.

Data Export Functionality

Export filtered listings outright to CSV formats. Take data representations forward by printing the entire dashboard out, converting visual graphics to structured PDF elements, or dynamically sharing intelligence summaries using modern Web Share integrations natively.
