Product Overview & Features

A comprehensive breakdown of the Law Firm Case Management System.

System Overview, Technology & Setup

  • Purpose: A centralized platform for legal professionals to manage cases, clients, tasks, and documents efficiently.
  • Technology Used: Built as a robust SharePoint (SPFx) application tailored to enhance organizational agility and compliance.
  • Initial Setup: Features an automated one-time configuration with a "Verify & Fix" setup wizard that instantly creates required SharePoint lists.
  • Deployment: Packaged as an SPPKG solution, deployed to the SharePoint Sub/Tenant App Catalog seamlessly.

Workflow & Details

Explore the intuitive structure of Case Management and its robust capabilities.

Case Management

Create, edit, and track legal cases with dynamic and detailed metadata attributes. Quickly view comprehensive case information directly from a centralized case details modal and seamlessly connect structured, pre-existing clients directly from dynamic dropdowns.

Clients Management

Maintain a comprehensive registry of securely contained client information accurately tied to their respective cases. Easily view, edit, and update client records utilizing integrated modal forms explicitly designed for rapid accessibility.

Tasks & Deadlines

Construct a robust timeline depicting relevant events, hearings, and fundamental case deadlines. Skillfully dispatch, supervise, and process ongoing organizational tasks allocated to dedicated operative roles.

Documents & Payments

Organize case-centric evidentiary and external documentation within an organized repository framework. Maintain complex audit logs of payments spanning total values met, respective balances, and verifiable transaction records.
