Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does TorridWave offer?

TorridWave offers comprehensive technology solutions including web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, technology consulting, cloud solutions, and ongoing support & maintenance services. We provide end-to-end solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website takes 4-8 weeks, while a web application can take 6-12 weeks. Mobile apps typically require 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the project.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models to suit different needs:

  • Fixed Price: For well-defined projects with clear requirements
  • Hourly Rate: For projects with evolving requirements
  • Retainer: For ongoing support and maintenance

Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages including:

  • 24/7 technical support
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Bug fixes and troubleshooting
  • Backup and recovery services
  • Feature enhancements and updates

We can customize a support plan that fits your needs and budget.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We're experienced in collaborating with in-house teams and can integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows and development processes. We can work as an extension of your team, providing expertise where needed, or take full ownership of projects.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies:

  • Frontend: React, Vue.js, Angular, Next.js, HTML5, CSS3
  • Backend: Node.js, Python, PHP, Java, .NET

  • Cloud: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform

We choose technologies based on your project requirements and long-term goals.
